European cars and their brands are among the top selling brands of vehicles in the world. As of summer 2021, the German car company Volkswagen is the second most earing motor company in the world falling only slightly behind Toyota. Europe is home to some of the most famous car brands in the world ranging from the most exotic cars ever created to cars that helped drive the Soviet Union through the cold war. In just Germany alone a few of the most popular luxury car brands in every continent are built such as BMW, Audi, and Mercedes-Benz. Italy is famous for their presence in the world of motorsports and designing the best track cars ever. Ferrari is based and founded in Italy and is constantly battling its biggest rival, Lamborghini, for the title of best supercars in the world. European cars are typically stylish, sporty, and compact to fit on tight city roads in densely populated areas. They continue to engineer cars with new and helpful technology to keep their reputation in the automotive world.

These are two important cars in Eruopean car culture

The Volkswagen Golf

The Golf currently the top selling car in all of Europe as of summer 2021 and has been near the top of the list for many years. The compact family by Volkswagen from Germany is stylish, practical, and fun to drive. It gets a combined thirty-three miles per gallon in the city and on the highway. It is the top selling car in Europe for many good reasons, even though its compact it still offers plenty of cargo room and is offered with a turbo charged engine giving it a respectable amount of power. It has been on the market since 1974 and will be here to stay for much longer in the future.

The Lamborghini Countach

The Countach is often regarded as the greatest car to ever come from Lamborghini and for many good reasons. Countach is directly translated to “wow” in Italian and that is what the car did when it was first debuted in the 70’s. The car itself had very poor aerodynamic design and was cramped on the inside but that wasn’t the intentions of the car, it was made to impress people with its looks and style. It was an icon of it’s time as still has a look that makes it very distinguishing even to today’s standards. It was built from 1974 to 1990 with only 1,983 built. As of 2022 Lamborghini is brining back the cars name with a new design and is going to cost $2,640,000 dollars.
